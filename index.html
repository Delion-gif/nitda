UNIVERSITY OF CALABAR

The University of Calabar Library is the main academic library of the University of Calabar, located in Calabar, Cross River State, Nigeria.[9] Established in 1975, the library has since grown to become one of the largest and most comprehensive academic libraries in Nigeria, serving as a resource for students, faculty, and researchers.

CROSS RIVER UNIVERSITY

the university of Cross River State also known as UNICROSS is a state-owned tertiary institution with four campuses spread across four Local Government Areas of the state.[2] The University was formerly known as the Cross River University of Technology (CRUTECH).

ARTHUR JARVIS UNIVERSITY

Arthur Jarvis University (AJU) is a private university in Akpabuyo, Calabar, Cross River State in Nigeria.

DUKE UNIVERSITY MEDICAL SCHOOL

The Duke University School of Medicine, commonly known as Duke Med, is the medical school of Duke University. It was established in 1925 by James B. Duke.

COLLEGE OF EDUCATION AKAMKPA

The College of Education, Akamkpa is a state government higher education institution located in Akamkpa, Cross River State, Nigeria. The current Provost is James Archibong. The Deputy Provost is Ugbong Benedict Igboh while the Registrar is Bernard Okon. The Bursar is Daniel Abip while the Librarian is James Odu.

FEDERAL COLLEGE OF EDUCATION OBUDU

The Federal College of Education, Obudu was established as a regular College on November 30, 1982 by the first Executive President of Nigeria, His Excellency, Alhaji Shehu Usman Aliyu Shagari. At inception, the College operated in a temporary site within the premises of Government Secondary School, Obudu.
